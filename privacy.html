Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27/05/2025

Thank you for using DeedPlus, a mobile application designed to help users calculate deed registration costs based on the provided plot value and deed type (e.g., Sale, Gift, Exchange, etc.). This app is developed using Jetpack Compose and is available for Android devices.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DeedPlus handles information and data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

DeedPlus does NOT collect or store any personal information from its users. We do not require account creation, login, or access to any personal or sensitive data on your device.

2. Use of the Internet

The app allows users to download deed-related PDF files from official or public internet sources. These files are accessed through internet connectivity and opened via an in-app or external PDF viewer.

We do not track or store the content of these downloads.

3. Use of AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect certain information for advertising and analytics purposes, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., model, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • Ad interactions

These data are collected and processed by Google, not by DeedPlus. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data.

4. Permissions

DeedPlus may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access – To allow downloading deed query PDF files.

These permissions are used solely for providing core functionality and are not used to collect any personal data.

5. Data Security

Since DeedPlus does not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data at risk of breach. All features operate securely within your device.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links or redirect users to third-party websites or documents (e.g., government PDF sources). These services are not operated by us. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
